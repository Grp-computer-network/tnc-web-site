College Health Facilities


At our institution, we prioritize the health and well-being of our students and staff. The college provides comprehensive healthcare services, ensuring students have access to timely medical assistance. Our healthcare services include routine check-ups, emergency medical aid, and basic health support, ensuring all students feel safe and cared for.

Available Health Services

  • 24/7 Nurse availability for emergency medical assistance.
  • General health check-ups, including routine physical exams and screenings.
  • Basic first aid for minor injuries and accidents on campus.
  • Mental health support with counseling services.
  • Health education and wellness programs to promote healthy living.

Our Health Care Team

Our health care team consists of a dedicated nurse who is always available to provide medical assistance and ensure the safety of our students. The nurse is highly trained and can handle basic medical needs as well as emergencies.
