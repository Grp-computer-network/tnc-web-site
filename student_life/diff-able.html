Facilities for Differently-Abled


Inclusive Health and Accessibility


Our institution is committed to providing accessible healthcare and facilities for differently-abled students and staff. We ensure that everyone has equal access to medical support, mobility assistance, and inclusive health services.

Available Facilities

  • Wheelchair-accessible medical rooms and restrooms.
  • Specialized health support for differently-abled individuals.
  • Elevators and ramps for easy mobility within the campus.
  • Sign language interpreters for medical consultations.
  • Mental health counseling tailored for differently-abled students.
